About Us


Hi!

We are the Cranes and Pandas! We love making cool crafts with paper and yarn so that others can enjoy! We’re Crane and Panda, two siblings who are passionate about crafting. We’re so excited to share our love for origami and crochet with you here!

As for me, Crane, my journey with origami started when I discovered how fun and amazing it is to fold things from your imagination. Whether it’s a cute animal, a pretty flower, or even something more complex, I love how paper folding allows us to create beautiful designs. It’s such a calming and creative process, and I want to show you how easy it can be to get started with origami, no matter your skill level. Whether you’ve never folded paper before or are already making your own creations, there’s always something new to try!

As for crochet, to me, Panda, it’s one of my favorite ways to relax and get creative. There’s something so satisfying about turning a simple piece of yarn into something warm and cuddly, like . We love making amigurumi (cute little crochet animals) and experimenting with all sorts of colorful yarns. Crochet has so many possibilities, and we’re always trying out new stitches and patterns to keep things exciting. It’s also a wonderful way to unwind after a busy day, and we believe anyone can learn to crochet with just a little patience and practice. On this blog, we’ll be sharing tutorials, patterns, tips, and inspiration for both origami and crochet. Our goal is to make these crafts easy and fun for everyone, no matter if you’re just starting or have been crafting for years. We’re here to help you learn, explore new ideas, and hopefully find some joy in creating your own handmade projects. We’re so glad you found us, and we hope you’ll join us on this crafting journey. Thanks for visiting, and we can’t wait to share more with you!


Happy crafting!


by the Cranes and Pandas
